About

A few words on what's inside.

  • I

    Speech to text, refined

    Whisper transcribes your voice, then a local AI polishes the output — cleaner phrasing, fewer filler words, intent preserved.

  • II

    Named shortcuts

    Say “my email” and it types your actual email. Define your own phrases — addresses, signatures, boilerplate — and speak them into any app.

  • III

    Personal dictionary

    Teach it your names, jargon, and acronyms. It learns them once and gets them right every time.

  • IV

    Local, end to end

    Both the transcription and the AI refinement run on your Mac. No accounts, no cloud, no usage limits.

Private

Everything runs on your machine. No servers, no cloud, no data ever leaves your Mac.

Fast

Powered by Whisper, running natively. No network round-trips, no waiting.

Simple

One shortcut. Start talking. Text appears wherever your cursor is.
